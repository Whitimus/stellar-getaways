Moon

Brace yourself for an extraterrestrial experience like no other. As you set foot on the lunar surface, be prepared to witness awe-inspiring wonders that defy imagination. Explore the magnificence of the Moon's iconic landmarks, from the towering peaks of the Mons Huygens to the breathtaking expanse of the Sea of Tranquility. Uncover the mysteries of the moon's craters, marvel at the tranquility of the Copernicus Observatory, and snap selfies against the backdrop of the awe-inspiring Earthrise. But that's not all! Take a tour of our state-of-the-art Moon Base, where pioneering scientists and explorers conduct groundbreaking research.
